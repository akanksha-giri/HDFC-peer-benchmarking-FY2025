# HDFC Bank – Peer Benchmarking Analysis (FY2025)

Benchmarked HDFC Bank's financial performance against three Indian private sector peers —
ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, and Kotak Mahindra Bank — using FY2025 Annual Report data.

## Key Findings

- **Asset Quality:** HDFC leads with the lowest Gross NPA (1.33%) in the peer group
- **Efficiency:** ICICI is the benchmark at CIR of 38.64%; HDFC follows at 40.51%
- **Profitability:** Kotak leads on NIM (4.96%), RoA, and RoE across the peer set
- **Funding:** HDFC's CASA ratio (34.80%) is the lowest — a watch area for FY2026
- **Capital:** Kotak strongest at Tier 1 of 21.10%; Axis the weakest at 15.07%
